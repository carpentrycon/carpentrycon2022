This calendar lists all of the sessions scheduled for CarpentryCon 2022.

The default is a list view of sessions, but you can also toggle to a calendar view. Times will display in your time zone. In the title of each session:

  • R indicates that the session has an attendance cap and requires separate registration in addition to general registration. A link to register is available in the event link.
  • An asterisk (*) indicates that the session is repeated. The additional times available are listed in the event link.
  • ES indicates a session presented in Spanish and EN indicates a session presented in English. ES/EN indicates a session with presentations in both languages. All sessions will include English captioning and may include additional translation services as requested through registration.

If you have any questions about the program, please email carpentrycon@carpentries.org.
