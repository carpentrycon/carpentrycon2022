CarpentryCon 2022 planning is underway! It will only be possible with the generous support of businesses and foundations who support our mission of teaching foundational computational and data science skills to researchers to safeguard the future of sound, open, reproducible science.

Sponsorship opportunities

There are many ways to support CarpentryCon 2022! Reserve your sponsorship and connect with the CarpentryCon organizers - contact us at carpentrycon@carpentries.org to discuss the options.
